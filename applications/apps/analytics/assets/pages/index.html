Home Analytics

Welcome to the demonstrator version of an analytics tool which summarises your organisation's profile in Research Data Australia and other ANDS services. The service currently reports on the quality and accessibility of content published through RDA as well as on its usage (eg page views, searches performed and data accesses).

Where applicable the tool also reports on your organisation's DOI activity and any coverage in the Thomson Reuter's Data Citation Index.

Reporting begins from December 2014.

The tool will be updated in response to your needs. Feedback is welcomed and can be provided to services@ands.org.au.
